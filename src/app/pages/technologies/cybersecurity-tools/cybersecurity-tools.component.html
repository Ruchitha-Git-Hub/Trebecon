Identity & Access Management

Controlling who has access to what—and when—is critical to data security. Trebecon delivers robust IAM solutions that enable secure, frictionless access across systems and users.

  • Single sign-on (SSO), Multi-factor authentication
  • Role-based access control and least privilege enforcement
  • Lifecycle management for user identities and credentials
  • Tools: Okta, Azure AD, Ping Identity, AWS IAM

We design IAM strategies that reduce risk, enhance user experience, and support regulatory compliance.
