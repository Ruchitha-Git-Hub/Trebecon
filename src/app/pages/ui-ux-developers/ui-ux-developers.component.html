UI/UX Developers

A UI/UX developer is responsible for creating intuitive, user-friendly, and visually appealing digital interfaces for websites and applications. This involves combining design principles with front-end development to deliver seamless and engaging user experiences that meet business goals and user needs.

Qualification: Any Graduation (B.Tech / B.E. in computer science or related field)

Job Description :

Required Skills & Qualifications:

  • 3-5 years of experience in UI/UX design and front-end development.
  • Proficiency in design tools like Figma, Adobe XD, Sketch, or Photoshop.
  • Strong knowledge of HTML5, CSS3 (including Flexbox and Grid), and JavaScript.
  • Hands-on experience with front-end frameworks like React.js or Angular (preferred).
  • Expertise in responsive design, cross-browser compatibility, and mobile-first development.
  • Understanding of UX research methods, usability testing, and wireframing.
  • Ability to create interactive prototypes and mockups for client presentations.
  • Familiarity with accessibility standards (WCAG) and performance optimization.
  • Experience using version control systems like Git and platforms like GitHub/GitLab.
  • Understanding of Agile design and development processes.

Roles & Responsibilities :

Key Responsibilities:

  • Design and develop intuitive and visually appealing user interfaces for web and mobile applications.
  • Translate user needs and business requirements into interactive, functional, and responsive UI components.
  • Collaborate with developers to ensure design feasibility and pixel-perfect implementation.
  • Conduct user research, create user personas, and perform usability testing.
  • Produce wireframes, user flows, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups.
  • Ensure design consistency and adherence to branding guidelines across all platforms.
  • Optimize designs for usability, performance, and accessibility.
  • Continuously gather feedback and iterate on designs to enhance user satisfaction.
